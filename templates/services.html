Acupuncturist, Serra Reid, L.AC., Dipl.O.M.

myheavenandearth.com

I have worked in the field of mind/body health care for fifteen years, both in private practice and as a consultant to the United Nations on public health policy campaigns. In 2008 I was awarded a double Master's of Science degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine (Acupuncture and Herbal Medicine), and upon passing the California State Board Exams was certified as a Licensed Acupuncturist (L.Ac.).

Additionally, I have the honor of being certified as a Diplomate of Oriental Medicine, which grants me privilege to practice Chinese Medicine throughout the United States.

I am passionately committed to helping create opportunities for people to heal at physical, emotional and spiritual levels. I have been trained in multiple medical modalities, both western and eastern, and enjoy using different perspectives to assess and treat health imbalances.
